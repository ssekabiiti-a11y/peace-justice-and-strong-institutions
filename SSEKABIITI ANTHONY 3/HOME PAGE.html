Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Objectives

Peace is the state of harmony, calm, and happiness without conflict. It can be found in your heart, in your community, and in your country.

Justice is about fairness and treating everyone equally. It means giving people what they deserve, respecting their rights, and ensuring honesty and fairness for all.

Strong institutions are reliable, organized systems that help societies function effectively — like rules and leaders that ensure people follow laws, protect each other, and cooperate.

Beneficiaries

  • Individuals
  • Communities
  • Societies
  • Nations and the global society

Benefits of Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

  • Fair treatment
  • Reduction of inequality
  • Good governance and leadership
  • Efficient public services
  • Long-term stability
  • Inclusive growth

Peace, justice, and strong institutions are not just ideals — they are the foundation of a better world.
